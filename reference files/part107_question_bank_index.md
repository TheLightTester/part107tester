## Topics Covered

### Module I — Regulations
Eligibility, UAS registration (Part 48), accident reporting (§107.9), certificate recency (§107.65),
daylight/civil twilight ops (§107.29), right of way (§107.37), airspace authorization (§107.41),
alcohol/drugs (§107.27), in-flight emergency (§107.21), falsification (§107.5), Remote ID (Part 89),
remote PIC responsibility (§107.19), hazardous operations (§107.23), carriage of hazardous material
(§107.36), waivers (§107.200/205), medical conditions (§107.17), condition for safe operation (§107.15),
FAA inspection (§107.7), multiple UAS (§107.35), NTSB reporting (49 CFR Part 830)

### Module II — Airspace & Charts
Class B/C/D/E/G airspace, special use airspace (P/R/W/MOA/Alert/CFA areas), military training
routes (MTRs), TFRs and NOTAMs (FDC NOTAMs), sectional chart symbols, obstacle notation
(MSL/AGL), VFR reporting points, CTAF/UNICOM frequencies, ATIS, latitude/longitude,
runway numbering, antenna towers, isogonic lines, Victor airways, private airports

### Module III — Weather
METAR decoding (wind, visibility, sky condition, modifiers), TAF decoding (FM/TEMPO/BECMG),
density altitude, atmospheric stability (stable vs. unstable), air masses and fronts, thunderstorm
life cycle, structural icing, low-level wind shear, temperature inversions, fog formation, mountain
weather, SIGMETs/AIRMETs, weather briefing sources (1800wxbrief.com), pressure altitude

### Module IV — Loading & Performance
Center of gravity, weight and balance, load factors, performance-degrading factors (H4: Heavy/Hot/
High/Humid), standard atmosphere (29.92 in Hg / 59°F), microbursts, humidity effects, temperature
effects, wind obstruction turbulence

### Module V — Operations
Operating limits (400 ft AGL, 100 mph, 3 SM vis, cloud clearances), VLOS requirements, visual
observers, traffic scanning technique, battery/LiPo safety, thermal runaway, ADM (3P model),
CRM (all phases), hazardous attitudes (anti-authority/impulsivity/invulnerability/macho/resignation),
PAVE checklist, IMSAFE checklist, operational pitfalls (get-there-itis, etc.), situational awareness,
SRM, emergency procedures, maintenance protocols, preflight inspection (§107.49), operations
over people (Category 1-4), operations from moving vehicles, 400-ft structure exception, TFR
violations, lost link procedures, alcohol effects, fatigue, hyperventilation, stress, dehydration,
medication (48-hour rule), NASA ASRS, crew briefing requirements, airport traffic patterns
